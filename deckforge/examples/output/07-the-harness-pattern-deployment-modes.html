Deployment Modes

There are three modes that matter for universities:

Supervised

Agent drafts, human approves.

Ideal for admissions, financial aid

Autonomous

Agent acts independently within strict guardrails.

Ideal for scheduling, FAQ responses

Collaborative

Agent and human work together in real-time.

Ideal for research assistance, advising sessions

The mode should be chosen based on the consequence of error, not the complexity of the task.
