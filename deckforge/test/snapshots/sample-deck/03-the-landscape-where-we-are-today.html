Where We Are Today

Every major university system is experimenting with AI, but most are stuck at the chatbot stage. The real opportunity is in agentic systems — AI that can take action, not just answer questions.

Admissions processing

agents that triage applications, flag anomalies, and draft reviewer summaries

Student advising

agents that understand degree requirements and proactively reach out when students fall behind

Research compliance

agents that monitor IRB submissions and flag issues before they become problems
