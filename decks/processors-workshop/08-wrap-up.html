Key takeaways

🛡️
Guardrails Are Just the Start
processInput stops bad messages, but processors also route models, enforce tool ordering, monitor drift, track costs, and trigger business logic on completion.
⚡
Performance Is a Design Choice
Regex first, safeguard models for classification, parallel checks, cheaper models per step. Don't pay for what you don't need.
🏗️
Compose Into Pipelines
.then(), .parallel(), .branch() — build layered defense systems from simple, testable processor building blocks.
